The problem of Insomnia
Insomnia is a wide spread and common issue within the first world countries. Insomnia is a condition in where a person has continuous issues with falling asleep. This is an issue as symptoms of sleep deprivation include reduced concentration, which can cause car crashes and other life threating accidents, and high blood pressure which can lead to diseases.
