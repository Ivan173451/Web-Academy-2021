Benefits of Resting

Just like all the emotions we feel, calmness can have an affect on our bodies. Some effects include lowered blood pressure, better sleep, less stress, and increased blood flow.

Some ways to get these benefits are through relaxation techniques. Some relaxation techniques are:

  • Yoga
  • Calming Music
  • Deep Breaths
  • Massages
  • Mediatation

The methods in the "Ways To Relax" page also works.
